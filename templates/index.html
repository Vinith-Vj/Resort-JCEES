Discover Kerala

Why Choose Kerala ?

Kerala, known as God’s Own Country, is a paradise of lush green landscapes, serene backwaters, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you seek adventure in the Western Ghats, a relaxing houseboat cruise in Alleppey, or the vibrant festivals and delicious cuisine, Kerala offers a unique and unforgettable travel experience.

Why Choose Tangasseri ?

Tangasseri, a charming coastal town in Kerala, is a hidden gem rich in history, culture, and scenic beauty. Known for its iconic lighthouse, historic churches, and serene beaches, this quaint destination offers a perfect blend of heritage and tranquility. Whether you're exploring its colonial past or enjoying the fresh seafood and warm hospitality, Tangasseri promises a unique and memorable experience.

Munnar

Munnar, a picturesque hill station in Kerala, is known for its lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and serene landscapes. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it offers breathtaking views, cascading waterfalls, and rich biodiversity. Visitors can explore Eravikulam National Park, trek to Anamudi Peak, or simply unwind amidst the cool, refreshing climate. Whether you're a nature lover or an adventure seeker, Munnar is a perfect retreat for a peaceful getaway

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is famous for its enchanting backwaters, houseboat cruises, and scenic beauty. Often called the "Venice of the East," this serene destination offers a unique experience of Kerala’s tranquil waterways lined with lush coconut palms and quaint villages. Visitors can enjoy a relaxing boat ride, witness the iconic Nehru Trophy Boat Race, and indulge in delicious Kerala seafood. Whether you're seeking peace or adventure, Alleppey is a must-visit destination.

Kochi (Cochin)

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant port city that blends history, culture, and modernity. Known as the Queen of the Arabian Sea, Kochi boasts colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and stunning waterfront views. Visitors can explore the iconic Chinese fishing nets, Fort Kochi’s charming streets, the historic Mattancherry Palace, and the lively Jew Town. With its rich spice trade heritage and diverse culinary scene, Kochi offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.
